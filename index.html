Our history

For many years bethany has been baking the most delicious pie at her home

In 2013, we opened first in brussels (Belgium) in a cosy little street near the Grand market. People all over the world fell in love with the soft cake, cheese cakes, pie cakes. The store became a success in no time

In 2015, no less than 100 pies were sold everyday and Bethany's pie shop moved into a large building. After recieving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making pies from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this, our very own online store, making it possible to order bethany's deliciuos.
